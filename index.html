The Fool

The Fool takes a creative approach to relationships. His carefree demeanor can be refreshing, but it's not always stable. If you're the fool, you're at risk of being overly influenced by those around you. If your partner is the fool, you may find that they don't fully appreciate what they have and approach love from a place of lightheartedness and immaturity.

The Fool is currently encouraging you to try a fresh approach to love. If you're single, try looking in new places. If you're in a relationship, it's time to take a risk, either letting go of your relationship or trying a bold move to take things deeper.
